01

Geographical

Island city-state in SouthEast Asia, 1'17N 103'50E.
It has a total land area of 724 square kilometres Singapore comprises of mainland and other islands.
It is separated from Indonesia by the Singapore Strait and from Malaysia by the Straits of Johor.

02

Political

The politics of Singapore takes the form of a parliamentary representative
democratic republic whereby the President of Singapore is the Head of State,
The Prime Minister of the state is the Head of Government and of a multi-party state.

03

Economical

The country's currency is Singapore dollar, Gross Domestic Product was stated to be 372.1 billion USD in 2019.
Key industry clusters in Singapore is manufacturing and electronics.
